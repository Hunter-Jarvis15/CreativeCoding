Here's The Projects I Made Over the Semester!

Self Portrait

This is my self portrait. We began working Java Script to get this to work.
We started out with creating simple shapes just by themselves and then slowly
got them to be 'drawn' where we wanted them with the coordinates based on the
size of our canvas. Once we were happy with how it looked, we were told to make
some of the shapes move left and right, and up and down. They also had to be
moving at varying speeds. We also got to change the colors of our shapes and of
our canvas which added to the overall picture.

Game

This is my game. After our self portrait, we continued to build on our skills in java script.
The first thing we created was our canvas. We got to design it however we wanted, so I went
with a blue and green color scheme for this. We then had to create a character, mine was a square,
and then add some enemies to the game, the gray circles. We had to move the square around our canvas
without dealing with collision between my character and enemies. Once I got my characterto move,
I made an exit that when my character reached it, text appears and congratulates you on exiting the board.
